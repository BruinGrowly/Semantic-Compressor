## After Successful Partnership

### Collaboration Agreement Template

Once a lab is interested, draft a collaboration agreement covering:

1. **Scope of Work**
   - Your contribution: Theory, analysis, manuscript drafting
   - Their contribution: Experiments, data collection, expertise

2. **Timeline**
   - Phase 1: Simulation (Month 1-2)
   - Phase 2: Initial experiments (Month 3-4)
   - Phase 3: Full validation (Month 5-6)

3. **Authorship**
   - Co-first authors: Theoretical lead + Experimental lead
   - Corresponding author: [Negotiable]
   - Other authors: All significant contributors

4. **IP & Data Sharing**
   - Publications: Joint copyright
   - Data: Shared access, archived publicly after publication
   - Future work: Right of first refusal for follow-up studies

5. **Funding**
   - Current: No funding required (lab resources)
   - Future: Include lab in grant proposals based on preliminary data

6. **Publication Strategy**
   - Target: Nature Physics (first choice), PRL (second choice)
   - Pre-print: arXiv before journal submission
   - Press release: Coordinate with both institutions
